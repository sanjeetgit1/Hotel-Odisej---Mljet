Set within the verdant pine and oak tree forest of Mljet National Park, Hotel Odisej Mljet is just two hours by passenger or car ferry from Dubrovnik.

Sweeping view of the Adriatic in your Room

Many rooms have balconies that beckon you to breathe in the pure Mediterranean air of the island. All rooms are air conditioned and feature an elegant ensuite bathroom with shower.

Dining options with regional specialities

A romantic sunset dinner, a quick afternoon snack, a relaxed family meal – our contemporary restaurants suit most occasions - and all with spectacular sea views.

Restaurant Odisej

The picture perfect panorama of Pomena Bay from the terrace draws a steady crowd to Restaurant Odisej. Every day a buffet breakfast is served, while the dinner menu is focused on regional seafood specialities.

Levanat Pizzeria

Overlooking the neatly lined boats at the very pretty Pomena marina, Levanat Pizzeria has enchanting views over the crystal-clear Adriatic. Sample from a menu of fresh pizza, lasagne, salads, and other Italian classics, not the least, delicious gelato.

Vista Mare Beach Bar

Enjoy a sublime stretch of gently shaded waterfront next to Pomena pier, where all the yachts and ships dock, Vista Mare Beach Bar serves cocktails, wine, ice cream and more at comfortable rattan sofas and large wooden tables.
